About Caesarspalace

Ceasarspalace is a world renowned casino located on the west side of the famous Las Vegas Strip. It is one of Las Vegas biggest and best know landmarks. Caesars palace was founded in 1966 by Jay Sarno and Stanley Mallin to give guests a sense of life during the Roman empire. Among the many statues and columns in the casion a 20-foot statue of Caesar stands near the entrance.
